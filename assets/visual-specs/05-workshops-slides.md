## Slide Design Guidelines

### Colors
- Primary: #3B82F6 (Blue)
- Secondary: #14B8A6 (Teal)
- Accent: #8B5CF6 (Purple)
- Warning: #EF4444 (Red)
- Success: #22C55E (Green)
- Background: #FFFFFF or #0F172A (dark)
- Text: #1E293B or #F8FAFC (dark)

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, large (48-72px)
- Body: Regular (24-32px)
- Code: Monospace (20-24px)

### Layout
- Clean, minimal
- One main idea per slide
- Visual > Text
- Consistent margins

---

## Variations Needed

1. **Full slide decks** - PowerPoint/Google Slides/Keynote
2. **PDF exports** - For handouts
3. **Dark mode** versions
4. **Branded** - Add company logo areas
5. **Printable** - High contrast for printing
