## Visual Style Guide

### Colors
- **Primary:** #2563EB (Blue)
- **Secondary:** #10B981 (Green)
- **Accent:** #F59E0B (Amber/Gold)
- **Warning:** #EF4444 (Red)
- **Background:** #F8FAFC (Light Gray)
- **Text:** #1E293B (Dark Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono

### Style
- Clean, minimalist
- Subtle shadows/depth
- Rounded corners (8px)
- Consistent iconography (Lucide/Heroicons style)
- Dark mode compatible
